# Welcome to My Blog

Hello there! Welcome to my personal blog where I share thoughts about software development, system architecture, and the Unix philosophy that guides my work.

## What You'll Find Here

This blog is an extension of my portfolio where I dive deeper into technical topics. You can expect to find:

### Development Insights
I'll share experiences from working with different technologies, particularly:
- **Backend Development**: Go, Node.js, system design
- **Frontend Development**: React, Next.js, TypeScript
- **DevOps**: Docker, CI/CD, infrastructure as code

### Architecture Thoughts
Systems thinking and architectural decisions that have shaped my projects.

### Unix Philosophy
How the principles of simplicity, composability, and clarity influence modern software development.

## The Terminal Aesthetic

As you might notice, this blog maintains the terminal aesthetic of my portfolio while being optimized for reading longer content. It's a balance between the Unix-minimalist philosophy I love and the practical needs of content consumption.
